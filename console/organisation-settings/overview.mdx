## Branding

This is where you can configure the whitelabel branding for your agency.

![Branding section](https://cdn.blutui.com/uploads/assets/Help/agency/organisation-settings/branding/brandin-new.png?format=webp&width=800)

This where you can set your `Logo`, `Primary colour` and `Secondary colour`.
This brand will then show up in the agency console, site dashboard and emails sent from the site to your clients.

## Team

This is where you invite your team into your agency.

![Team section](https://cdn.blutui.com/uploads/assets/Help/agency/organisation-settings/creating-a-role/screenshot-2026-01-15-at-93717-am.png?format=webp&width=800)

Before inviting someone to your agency, you must have an available seat. Seat availability is displayed at the top of the Team page, showing your total seats, current members, and remaining seats.
To invite a new team member, click Invite member.
If you don't have any remaining seats, click Add seats (next to Invite member) to open the seats management panel and add additional seats before continuing with the invitation.

![Add seats](https://cdn.blutui.com/uploads/assets/Help/agency/organisation-settings/creating-a-role/screenshot-2026-01-15-at-93744-am.png?format=webp&width=800)

You send an invite by typing in the `Email address` of the person you are trying to invite into your team. They will recieve an email with a link to join the agency.

![Send invitation](https://cdn.blutui.com/uploads/assets/Help/agency/organisation-settings/creating-a-role/screenshot-2026-01-15-at-93733-am.png?format=webp&width=800)

Select the correct `Role` for the invite, these couls be `Owner`, `Admin`, `Developer` or a custom role you created earlier.
