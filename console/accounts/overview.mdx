## Organisations

### Blutui will automatically create and add your user to an organisation when you sign up

Initially you are the sole owner of your organisation but you can add other Blutui users to your organisation at the following user levels:

- Owner
- Admin
- Developer

Permission configurations for the above can be modified using the 'Roles' settings in the Agency Console.

`Settings > Roles`

### Client Accounts

#### Add as many client-side account as you need with Blutui

Blutui allows you to add as many client-side accounts as you need to a site from the Dashboard. Client-side accounts include Site Owners and Content Managers.

- Clients and Content Managers can log into the site by appending /admin_ to their domain name, for example access to the dashboard of site.com would be via https://site.com/admin
- Blutui agency and developer users with access to the Agency Console can use their main account details to log into the Dashboard.
- You can create client accounts from the site dashboard at: Settings > User Accounts > Users
- When you create a client account they will be sent an invite email with a token based link, on following the link they will be asked to set a username and password for the site.
- Following sign up clients will be asked to verify their email address via a second email.

<Note>
  The Dashboard is different from the Agency Console. The Agency Console is the interface you are directed to when you login, whereas the Dashboard needs to be logged into separately.
</Note>

## Billing

You can view your billing details at any point from your organisation's drop down box. In order to maintain an active Blutui account you must keep a valid credit card on file.

- Blutui will charge all fees to the card on file, this card will be charged for any additional services or purchases such as domains or images.
- If the card fails your sites will remain online and your collaborators, site owners and content managers will remain associated with your accounts.
- Blutui provides a 4 week period to correct any billing issues before services are disrupted.
- You will be notified by email if there are any problems with your billing.
- If service is interrupted for billing reasons it will be restored as soon as the billing issue is resolved.
- Any overdue fees will be charged as soon as a billing issue is resolved
